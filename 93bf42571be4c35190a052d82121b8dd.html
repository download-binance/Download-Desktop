We take our urchins for a spin

I love spinach, especially good old-fashioned ones. When there is a holiday for which there will be a Piglet, we will take our puff. Sometimes, especially hot spring, it is also good to take a chicken, puff it, and tie it up. For some reason, I, as you know, I prefer spinach. In fact, although there are many variations, I find it easiest to use puff pastry. Simply pour a thin layer of butter on top of the dough and carefully fold it in half. bake at 180 degrees until Golden. While the filling is baking, put the chicken in the oven, prepare the salad. Let the pies cool. The family liked the taste of sour cream and was happy to eat puff pastry. Sometimes I cook such a dish, which is always very tasty. The family liked that the puffs were greasy and very tasty. The flavor was not felt in the hands, but in the taste of the host. It was a shame when they ate them with sour cream sauce.
